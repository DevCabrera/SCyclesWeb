Squat Routines

Russian


This program is designed to increase strength but not mass. Not intended to be the only squatting program in a routine, and shouldn't be used as the only squating routine. It is a 6 week, 3 days per week program and a great beginning squat program.

Smolov Jr.


This is a high volume and high frequency routine that last only 3 weeks, spread 4 days per week, or 3 days per week. This is a more intermediate routine designed for more experienced lifters, those looking to improve a lift, or those that want a high volume and frequency routine.

Smolov


This is one of the most challenging squat programs, and claims to help boost a persons squat by up to 100 lbs. in just 13 weeks. Consists of a variation between 2-4 days a week. Designed for those that are experienced, have proper squat technique, and can squat at least 300 lbs.
